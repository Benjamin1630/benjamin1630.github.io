Who am I?

About Myself

My name is Benjamin Reding and I was born in Luxembourg.
Born in 1999 so I am fortunate enough to have grown up alongside the gaming industry.
I have been playing games since the time of the gamecube and have played countless games since then.
Very proactive in keeping up-to-date with the latest and greatest in the industry.
I am a strong creative and a hard worker.
My interest for gaming and the industry surrounding it has always been my favorite.


My History

I was born and raised in Luxembourg where I spent my first 20 Years.
At 17 I was moving between schools trying to find the right course for games programming, but unfortunately at this time, there were no such courses, but I still spent a good 4 years picking up skills and learning regardless.
Since there were no courses, I looked beyond the borders and went to college in the UK where I studied Games development for 3 years.
I then moved to the Netherlands to study Creative Media & Games Technology at a University Bachelor level.


My Future

In my future, I aim to either start my own studio, or lead a team that continues to innovate and bring experiences that focus on the user first.


My Passion

It is very important to me that a game feels good to play.
I am extremely passionate about what I do.
I take great care and put in extra effort in every project I am a part of.
I aim to bring a pleasant and satisfying feel alongside a polished experience to everything I make.
