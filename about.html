Who am I?

My History

I was born and raised in Luxembourg where I spent my first 20 Years.

Most of my late teens were spent moving between schools trying to find the right course for games programming, but unfortunately at this time, there were no such courses, but I still spent a good 4 years picking up skills and learning regardless.

Since there were no courses, I looked beyond the borders and went to college in the UK where I studied for 3 years.

I then moved to the Netherlands to study at a University level.


My Future

In my future, I aim to either start my own studio, or join a team that continues to innovate and focus on the user first.


My Passion

I aim to bring a pleasent and satisfying feel alongside a polished experience to everything I make.
It is very important to me that a game feels good to play.
